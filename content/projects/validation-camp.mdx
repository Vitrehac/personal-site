## Overview

In August 2024, I participated in the **Validation Camp** organized by **Start IT @ČSOB** — one of the leading startup accelerator programs in the Czech Republic.

## The Startup: ExpectUp

**ExpectUp** is a B2B platform designed to simplify ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) reporting for small and medium enterprises.

### The Problem

ESG reporting is becoming mandatory for more and more companies across Europe, yet the process remains complex, expensive, and inaccessible — especially for smaller businesses that lack dedicated sustainability teams.

### Our Approach

We set out to build a tool that would make ESG compliance as straightforward as possible — think guided workflows, automated data collection, and plain-language reporting.

## What I Learned

- How to validate a B2B product idea in weeks, not months
- Customer discovery techniques for enterprise clients
- Pitching to investors and industry mentors
- The intersection of regulation, technology, and sustainability
